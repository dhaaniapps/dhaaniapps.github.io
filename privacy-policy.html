Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 26, 2026

Welcome to Kids Drawing Fun. We develop this application as a Ads Enabled Free app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information for users of our mobile application.

Google Play Families Policy Compliance: This app is designed for children under the age of 13. We strictly adhere to the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We do not knowingly collect, transmit, or share any personal data or persistent identifiers from children.

1. Information Collection and Use

We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). For a better experience while using our Service, we do not require you to provide us with any personal data.

  • No Personal Identifiers: We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or precise location data.
  • No Device/Persistent Identifiers: We do not collect or transmit Android Advertising IDs, MAC addresses, or IMEI numbers, ensuring full compliance with Google Play's restrictions for children's apps.
  • Local Storage: All drawings, artwork, and creative data remain strictly on your physical device and are never uploaded to any external cloud or server.

2. Third-Party Services and SDKs

To ensure a safe environment for children, our application does not integrate any third-party Software Development Kits (SDKs) that track user behavior, serve personalized ads, or transmit data. There are no social media plug-ins or public sharing features available within the app.

3. Security

We value your trust in providing us your children's creative space. Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security depends on your device's built-in operating system protections. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all local application data.

4. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13 by collecting data. If you are a parent or guardian and you learn that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately so we can take necessary actions to remove it from our logs.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us.
