Zip File Manager mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user data and details our commitment to data safety.

By installing and using this App, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information Collection and Use

Our primary objective is to provide a local file compression and management tool. We design the App to handle files directly on your device.

A. Personal Data

  • No Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). This includes names, email addresses, phone numbers, or physical addresses.

B. File Data and Storage

  • Local Processing: The App requires access to your device's storage to view, compress (ZIP), and decompress (UNZIP) files.
  • Privacy Assurance: All file processing happens entirely on your local device. We never upload, stream, or transmit your files, folders, or document contents to any external servers.

C. Automatically Collected Data

2. Device Permissions Required

To function as a file manager, the App must request specific sensitive device permissions. We use these permissions strictly for core utilities:

  • Read/Write Storage Permission (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Required on older Android versions to read files for archiving and saving extracted directories.
  • All Files Access (MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE): Required on Android 11+ to manage, zip, rename, and clear files across your device's shared storage volumes.
