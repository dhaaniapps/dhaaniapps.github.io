Privacy Policy for Guardian Notepad AI

Effective Date: June 20, 2026

Updated Date: June 28, 2026

Guardian Notepad AI ("the App"), developed by Dhaani Apps, is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

A. Personal Notes and Data

  • Local Storage: Your notes, folders, and tags are stored locally on your device in a secure SQLite database. We do not host your notes on our servers.
  • Backup & Restore: If you use the "Backup Data" feature, a copy of your database is saved to a location of your choice on your device or personal cloud storage. This data is managed entirely by you.

B. Guardian Emergency Feature

If you choose to use the optional "Guardian" emergency feature, the app collects and stores:

  • Email Addresses: Used to send automated alerts via SMTP to your designated contacts.
  • Phone Numbers: Used to send automated emergency SMS to your chosen recipients.
  • Storage: This data is stored locally on your device within a secure database.
  • Transmission: Data is transmitted off-device only when an emergency trigger is activated (e.g., an unresponsive reminder). At that point, the data is sent to the mail server (via SMTP) or SMS gateway to notify your chosen contacts.
  • Privacy: We do not use this information for marketing or share it with any third parties beyond the necessary transmission to notify your emergency contacts.

C. AI Features (Summarization and OCR)

  • Processing: AI Summarization and Tagging features use the Google Generative AI SDK. While processing, text from your notes may be sent to Google servers to generate the summary or tags.
  • OCR: Handwriting and text recognition (OCR) is processed using Google ML Kit, which primarily performs on-device processing.

D. Biometric Data

  • Security: The App uses the Android Biometric library for the App Lock and Note Lock features. We do not have access to your actual fingerprint or facial data; the App only receives a "Success" or "Failure" confirmation from the Android system.

2. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to provide personalized ads. We use the User Messaging Platform (UMP) to handle GDPR and privacy consent.
  • Google Play Services: Used for app infrastructure and performance monitoring.

3. Data Safety

In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety requirements:

  • Data Collected: Contact info (Phone numbers and Email addresses if using Emergency features). This data is stored locally on your device. App interactions and Device IDs (for ads) are also collected.
  • Data Shared: Device IDs are shared with third-party advertising partners (AdMob). Emergency contact info is shared only with the relevant gateways (SMS/SMTP) upon user-triggered emergency events.
  • Security: All data is handled using modern Android security standards.

4. Permissions Used

  • Internet: To load AI features and ads.
  • SMS: To send emergency alerts to your designated contacts.
  • Contacts: To allow you to pick emergency recipients.
  • Camera: To take photos for OCR (Handwriting to Text).
  • Biometrics: To secure your private notes.
  • Alarms: To ensure reminders trigger exactly on time.

5. Children’s Privacy

Our App does not specifically target children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can delete your notes or clear the app's data at any time through your device settings, which will permanently remove all information stored by the App.
