About KidsPaint Studio

A creative and educational app designed for kids to draw, color, and learn letters and numbers in a fun, safe environment. KidsPaint Studio combines artistic expression with educational learning, making it perfect for toddlers and young children.

Key Features

🖌️ Paint & Draw

Free drawing canvas with colorful brushes, multiple colors, and drawing tools that encourage creativity and imagination.

🔤 Learn ABCs

Trace and learn uppercase and lowercase letters with guided drawing activities.

🔢 Learn 123s

Explore numbers with interactive number chart and counting exercises.

🎨 Coloring Pages

Fun coloring pages to entertain kids while developing fine motor skills.

🔒 Parental Lock

Lock the app to prevent kids from accidentally exiting. Parents can unlock anytime from the menu.

✨ Safe for Kids

No personal data collection, no login required. Designed with your child's safety in mind.
